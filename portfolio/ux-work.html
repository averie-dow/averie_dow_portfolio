CU User Experience Team

I currently work as an Undergraduate UI/UX Designer for the User Experience Team at the CU Boulder Office of Information Technology. In this role, I collaborate with industry professionals and CU campus offices to develop user-friendly and accessible digital interfaces for all users. Using tools like Figma, I design and refine new digital interfaces and improve existing campus products. I follow WCAG standards for accessibility, and test my tools using color contrast, screenreader, and HTML Validators.

I most frequently design for Buff Portal, which is the CU Student Portal for managing registration, finances, and additional campus information. I also ideate for individual campus offices to improve current experiences.

UConnect

After experiencing the frustration of finding friends, roommates, and extracirriculars at a large school, we wanted to create an application that bridged the gap between potential roommates, clubs, and peers. UConnect removes the third party messengers and planners each campus group uses to connect students.

How Might We... ensure that CU Boulder students have an authentic, reliable, and safe way to communicate with potential roommates, peers, and student organizations?

Identifying our key stakeholders in the housing, clubs, and friends finding process at CU Boulder.

After conducting user experience interviews, we coded the data in Figma and used this qualitative information to inform our design process.

Created lo-fidelity prototypes for our final project and included a proposed task flow, as well as did usability testing in Figma.
